Minimal devices

Showcase your products in device mockups


Minimal devices let you showcase your product preview in device mockups such iPad, iPhone, iMac and the macbook. You can also add glare effect and increase the mockup size as much as you want.

Its built by Jared hardy. You can check out each device code easily through its clean and simple UI at its official website.


Voskan modifications

We have further added features into minimal devices by making it responsive. You can use the block-3-macbook class on the md-macbook-pro div to make it responsive. Likewise, you use the block-3-imac class on the md-imac div. This feature is derived from block 3.

For the iPhone and the iPad we have the block 6.
